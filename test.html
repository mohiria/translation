The lead scientist sat by the window as the wind began to blow through the autumn leaves. He held a record of the minute changes he had recorded in the local environment. His mind was not content with the current content of the report, as he suspected a leak of lead into the river bank near the park.

Suddenly, he noticed a rose in a glass jar on his table. As the sun rose higher, a single tear escaped his eye. He had to tear himself away from these memories to focus on his advanced research. He knew he must match his firm beliefs with perfect implementation to improve the situation.

He decided to close the file and take a second to watch the match outside. A bird began to fly over the plant nursery, its wings flapping in a rhythmic wave. This kind of natural sound always improved his mood and helped him will himself to continue his long-term project.
